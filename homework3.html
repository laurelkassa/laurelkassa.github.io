Review of States

  1. Vermont

    2. This is by far my favorite website. The search bar is front and center so it is easy to look around and maneuver. There is a good amount of pull tabs so I can choose what I want to look for. There is a nice amount of photos and they are pretty and it makes it look like Vermont is a place you would want to visit. The website loaded quickly and was ready for me to use. The screen looks nice on a computer, probably the best of all of the websites.

  2. Alaska

    3. This website was the fastest of all of them to load for me. It quickly had everything up and that was pleasing. There is a lot going on in this website, but it is easy to maneuver. I am able to find what I am looking for, but there is a lot of extra words on this webpage. I think it looks pretty nice but they could have had different pages with words, rather than all on the front page. This website looked nice on a computer, it fit well on the screen. The first photo on Alaska's website is very pretty- I think that gives Alaska a nice aesthetic look.

  3. Oklahoma

    4. I like the top of this website, it looks aesthetic to me. This seems like an old website to me, like they have not updated it in a long time. They need to add a lot. I can find what I am looking for on the search bar, but there is no pull down tabs so I just have to search what I am looking for which I do not mind. The website looks nice on a computer, but there is some awkward space on the sides so they could change that to make it look better.

  4. Tennessee

    5. This website was quick to load, not the fastest, it was just pretty average. I think the colors are not as pleasing to me, it seems kind of dark to me. But I do like how they have the switching photos on the front page. I enjoy how there is not a ton of words right as you get on the front page, but there is later when you scroll. The website also looks nice on a computer, it fills the entire screen. I think the photos they used could have been much better because Tennessee is a pretty place but the photos they used didn't do it justice.

  5. Iowa

    6. At first glance this website is very boring. They do not have very many colors or photos. It is easy to maneuver and find what you are looking for but a website needs to have more than that. Because it doesn't have a lot going on it could be easy follow but it is my least favorite website for that reason. Luckily this website looked nice on a computer but since the computer screen is so big it is really empty. They need to add a lot to this website.
