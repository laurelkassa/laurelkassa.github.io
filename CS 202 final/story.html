Our Story!

Lolo’s Granola began in the humble kitchen of my family’s home on Mountain View Lane. The intent was to sell my granola to fundraise enough money for my eight grade class trip to Washington DC. The granola was such a hit, that it helped me raise ALL my money, but it didn’t stop there! After my trip, orders continued to flow in, and have yet to stop eight years later!
